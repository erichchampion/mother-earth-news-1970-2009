Bug-Free Gloves

A clear plastic tennis ball container with a plastic top to store rubber work and garden gloves

By Arthur Lee

August/September 2000

When storing her rubber work and garden gloves, my wife puts them into a clear plastic tennis ball container with a plastic top. This keeps her gloves free of spiders when they are stored in dark places, under the sink or in her garden tool shed.
