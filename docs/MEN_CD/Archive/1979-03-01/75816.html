Mr. Digwell

By the Mother Earth News editors

March/April 1979

The cartoon feature below, Mr. Digwell-as you may or may not know-was originally created for
readers of the London Daily Mirror...wich explains why its advice is often couched in peculiarly British
terms. Despite the occasionally strange language, however, we think you'll find Mr. D's gardening tips
interesting and useful on this side of the pond too.
