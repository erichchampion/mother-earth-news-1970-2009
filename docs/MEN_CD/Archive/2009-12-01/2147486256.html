Greeting Card Organizer: Never Forget To Send The Very Best

In January, organize your greeting cards for the coming year.

By Donna Beaver

December 2009/January 2010

Here is a hint for remembering to send birthday and anniversary cards on time. At the start of the new year, I count all of the birthdays and anniversaries for the year, and purchase the cards. Then I address the cards and put the date the card is to be mailed in the corner where the stamp will be.

I put the cards in order by month and check the pile at the beginning of each month. When itâ€™s time to mail the card, I simply write a short greeting, sign the card, affix the stamp and pop it right into the mailbox. This greeting card organizer takes a bit of work in January, but the reward is the ease of sending cards the rest of the year.
