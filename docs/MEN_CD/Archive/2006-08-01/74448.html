GREEN GAZETTE

By Tabitha Alterman

If you want to educate your community about important environmental and social issues, you’ll find an ally in Bullfrog Films. This documentary film company offers hundreds of award-winning, independent films, such as “Affluenza,”“Design with the Sun” and “Deconstructing Supper,” that will inspire viewers to consider the welfare of all of Earth’s inhabitants. To request a free catalog of films to rent or purchase, call (800) 543-3764 or visit http://www.bullfrogfilms.com. Community groups receive a discount.
