A small bag can be carried on camping trips (lay it in the sun, and have a hot-water washup in the evening), while larger ones can heat water for pools or for domestic use. Write the Solar Sense Division of Inflatable Technology Corp., Dept ME, P.O. Box 9307, Providence, R.I. 02940 for prices and information.

COOK FISH JUST RIGHT:

A little device called Perfect Fish is now on the market to help you gauge just the right amount of time to cook any kind of fish-whole, in fillets or steaks, fresh or frozen-by measuring the fish at its thickest part. The Perfect Fish measuring scale, based on a method developed by the Canadian Department of Fisheries, precisely indicates temperatures and times for baking, broiling, deep-frying, panfrying, poaching, and grilling. Look for this useful item in the cookware section of major department stores, or send $3.95 to Williams-Sonoma, Dept. ME, P.O. Box 7456, San Francisco, Calif. 94120.

THREAD 'EM UP, TAKE'EM OUT:

POP brand tools and rivets are well known for quality and usefulness, and the company's threaded rivets are particularly handy for the do-it-yourselfer. These rivets provide a secure mount, and one that's easily removed later. They can be used for storm windows and doors, numbers or nameplates on aluminum-sided houses or mobile homes, license plates on vehicles, awnings, bicycle baskets, and so on. Installed properly, the threaded devices give the same secure blind fastening as do regular rivets. Ask for them at your local hardware or building supplies store.
