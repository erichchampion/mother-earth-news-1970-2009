A Thawing Idea

December/January 2000

Fresh air gives line-dried clothes the best scent I can imagine. During our long winter months, however, I froze my hands fighting with clothespins that froze to my lines. An elderly neighbor offered me a hint to my chilling problem: brine. Could it be so simple? Yes, it was. One soak a year in a strong brine solution keeps my clothespins working throughout our raw Vermont winters. It's a pure delight to have "fresh-air clothes" without frozen fingers or pins that "hibernate."

DEBBRA PROVOST
Sheffield, Vermont
