Bee Sting Salve

Country Lore: The best home remedy for bee, wasp and bumble bee stings is an onion.

By Teresa McSween

October/November 2000

The best home remedy for bee, wasp and bumble bee stings is to cut an onion in half and apply it right to the sting. After a few moments of slightly sharper stinging, the pain will diminish almost immediately, and the sting will heal in a short while. I always keep an onion in my first aid kit when traveling, camping, etc.

Teresa McSween
