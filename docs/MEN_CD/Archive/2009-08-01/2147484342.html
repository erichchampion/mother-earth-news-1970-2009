Free, Unique Scrapbooking Supplies

Greeting card pictures and textured paper make great scrapbooking materials.

By Jessica Cadeau

August/September 2009

While we were going through our wedding cards, it dawned on me that I could reuse them for scrapbooking supplies. In addition to the lovely pictures, some of the cards had poems inside that I could cut out. Some even had a vellum layer that could be carefully taken off and reused.

All of this can be done with birthday, graduation and holiday cards as well.

Jessica Cadeau
Newberry, Michigan

Recycled cards also make great gift tags. - MOTHER
