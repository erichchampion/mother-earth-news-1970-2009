Western Sheep Ranchers (2)

By the Mother Earth News editors

September/October 1973

Who illegally hire helicopters to hunt down golden eagles should know that, according to Kenya (Africa) biologist Dr. Leslie Brown, "There is massive, solid evidence that golden eagles pose no threat to sheep operations. Indeed, the birds may be indirectly beneficial in that eagles subsist largely on jack rabbits and other species which are in the potential competition for sheep forage."
