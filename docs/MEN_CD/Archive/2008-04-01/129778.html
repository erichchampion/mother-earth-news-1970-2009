Paintbrush Seed Sowing

Cover tiny seeds with a fine paintbrush.

By Norma Howie

April/May 2008

Some vegetable seeds are tiny and difficult to cover with your hand or a trowel. I have found that using a paint brush allows me to brush just the right amount of soil onto the seeds without disturbing them or planting them too deeply.

Norma Howie
Elmwood, Ontario
