See Solar Homes Nationwide

by Megan Phelps

October/November 2007

Every fall, the American Solar Energy Society organizes the National Solar Tour, with hundreds of coordinated events across the United States. Find an event near you, and you can tour green buildings and learn more about how people in your community use renewable energy. The official tour date is Saturday, Oct. 6, but events are held from September to November. You can find a list of all scheduled events at nationalsolartour.org. Plus, we’re happy to announce you can now experience these inspiring homes year-round, and see more than those just in your area. Homeowners and tour organizers are posting photos on our new photo-sharing Web site, CU.
