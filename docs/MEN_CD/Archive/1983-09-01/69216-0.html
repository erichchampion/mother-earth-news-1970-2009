PHOTOS BY THE AUTHOR
1] Pull each rabbit hide into a knife embedded in a stump, to produce an inch-wide strip of fur. [2 and 3] Now, twist these hide belts until only fur shows ... and hang them to dry. [4 and 5] Then begin the weaving process ... by working cords through the strips of fur to form a blanket. [6] The finished product is similar to that used by many early Native Americans.
