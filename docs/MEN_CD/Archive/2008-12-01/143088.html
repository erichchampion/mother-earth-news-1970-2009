Country Lore: Use Vinegar To Zap Hard Water Stains

Vinegar helps to keep your bathroom and kitchen free of hard water stains and buildup.

By Linda Johnson

December 2008/January 2009

If you have hard water, white vinegar makes a good toilet bowl cleaner to remove the hard water ring, and it leaves behind no toxic fumes. Vinegar also has some germ killing properties.

Linda Johnson
Early, Iowa
