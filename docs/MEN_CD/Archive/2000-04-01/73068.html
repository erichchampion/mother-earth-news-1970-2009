Extracting Mosquitoes

By Joan Chevalier

April/May 2000

An old country formula to ward off gnats and mosquitoes is to mix a tablespoon of vanilla extract into a cup of water. I rubbed this on my children's faces and it worked so well I tried the solution on my horse's ears and eyes. It worked like a charm.

Joan Chevalier, McEwan, TN
(August 1987)
