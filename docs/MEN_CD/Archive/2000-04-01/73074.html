Just One Whiff

By the Mother Earth News editors

April/May 2000

To keep cats, rabbits or other rodents out of your garden or flower bed, sprinkle cayenne pepper on the perimeter of the area you want pro tected. One whiff, and these critters will not be the least bit interested.

Use bird feeders to help eliminate the ant population in your yard by positioning the bird feeder above the ant nest. In a few days the nest will be much reduced. One last hint: Lemon halves rubbed onto your white porcelain sink will keep it white and free of mold and mildew. After cleaning, let stand for an hour or more before using the sink.

Bindy Beck-Meyer, Novato, CA
(January 1992)
