Stay Fresh Flowers

Country Lore: Favorite tips for keeping flowers fresh with sugar and removing a spot of rust from knives, with an onion.

By Linnea Lentz

April/May 2001

I am a recent subscriber to Mother Earth News and would like to pass along some of my favorite tips:

1. To help cut flowers stay fresh longer, add one tablespoon sugar and one drop of bleach to a quart of water. The bleach helps fight bacteria, and the sugar provides energy to the flowers.

2. If your knife has a spot of rust on it, stick the knife into an onion for 30 minutes and the spot will disappear.

LINNEA LENTZ
Stacy, Minnesota
