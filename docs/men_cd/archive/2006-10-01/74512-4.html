POTENTIAL C02 REDUCTIONS FROM ELECTRIC AND PLUG-IN ELECTRIC VEHICLES. Even if we switch from gasoline-powered cars to electric cars charged by existing utility grids (which mostly use fossil fuels), there would be significant reductions in carbon dioxide emissions, the primary greenhouse gas that leads to global warming. The map above shows the potential reductions for each state; the national average reduction would be 42 percent.
