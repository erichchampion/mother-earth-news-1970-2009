Willie Nelson's Biodiesel

November/December 2005

Willie Nelson, the 71-year-old Texas music legend longassociated with American farmers, has become an energy companyexecutive. But the difference between his company and the energygiants is that it supports American farms and clean, renewableenergy. In December 2004, Nelson and four business partners formeda company called Willie Nelson's Biodiesel that is marketing thefuel to truck stops. The clean-burning product called BioWillie ismade from American-grown vegetable oils, mainly soybeans, and canbe burned without modification in regular petro-diesel engines.Oklahoma City-based Love's Travel Stops and Country Stores willcarry the fuel at its 169 locations nationwide. Tractor-trailersare the largest group of diesel consumers in the country. Go toWillie Nelson's Biodieselfor more information.
