Vermicultural Tidbits

June/July 2000

To create the best organic material for a garden, build a 4 x 4 enclosure, add potting soil and fill this type bed with earthworms. Feed the earthworms table scraps and cornmeal and the worms will multiply quickly. Within a short period of time the soil will become enriched and can be used for your gardens. Earthworms consume a large amount of foodstuff daily, giving you a use for your table scraps.

Some cities are now considering using earthworms in their trash dump areas, since each worm eats its weight in scrap daily.
