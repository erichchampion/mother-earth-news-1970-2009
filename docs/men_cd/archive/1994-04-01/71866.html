Dick Sellers Finished His Dream

Mother reader completes his log cabin.

By the Mother Earth News editors

April/May 1994

Log Cabin Update

When MOTHER told Dick's story in "I Built a Log Cabin from Scratch for Under $11,000" [issue #137], his home was near completion. Just a few months ago, the last of the furniture and fixtures went in. Dick confesses that "the $1,200 septic tank put the final figure closer to $12,000." He's forgiven.
