Chocolate-Dipped Strawberries Recipe

Strawberries. Chocolate. Melted butter. Need we say more? Learn how to make chocolate covered strawberries.

By Roger Doiron

June/July 2009

Ingredients:
6 cups whole strawberries, unhulled, washed and dried
11⁄2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1 tbsp butter
Nuts, chopped (optional)

Instructions:
Line large baking sheet with waxed paper. Melt chocolate chips and butter in double boiler set over simmering water until mixture is smooth. Stir in a tablespoon or two of water if your chocolate is too thick. Holding strawberry by its green top, dip 3/4 of berry into melted chocolate. Let excess chocolate drip off into the boiler. Roll in chopped nuts, if using. Place on waxed paper. Repeat with remaining strawberries. Refrigerate until chocolate coating is firm, about 1 hour. Serves 6 to 8.
