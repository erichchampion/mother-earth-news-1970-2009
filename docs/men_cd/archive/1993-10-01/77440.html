The Last Laugh

By the Mother Earth News editors

October/November 1993

In April, we printed a photograph sent in by reader Kenneth D. Love in "Dear Mother." Remember the moose in the pool? Well, we so liked the idea of printing photos taken by readers that we asked you to send in your most prized, most ridiculous pictures. So far we've received about a dozen, and these six were our favorites. Now we're looking for more, so get out your cameras, start rummaging through your scrapbooks and send them to "Funny Fotos," c/o Mother Earth News P. O. Box 129, Arden, NC 28704.
