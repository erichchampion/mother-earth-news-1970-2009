Mr. Digwell

By the Mother Earth News editors

November/December 1981

The cartoon feature below, Mr. Digwell-as you may or may not know-was originally created for readers of the London Daily Mirror . . . which explains why its advice is often couched in peculiarly British terms. Despite the occasionally strange language, however. we think you'll find Mr. D's gardening tips interesting and useful on this side of the pond too.
