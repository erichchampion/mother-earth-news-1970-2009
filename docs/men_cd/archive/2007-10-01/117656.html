Home Power Turns 20

Celebrating 20 years of expert advice on every form of renewable energy.

By Cheryl Long

October/November 2007

Congratulations to Home Power magazine on its 20th anniversary. Check it out at their Web site, especially if you find yourself wanting more detailed, nuts-and-bolts articles about renewable energy than we can provide in Mother Earth News. Richard and Karen Perez and the Home Power team deliver expert advice on how to install all kinds of solar, hydro and wind power.
