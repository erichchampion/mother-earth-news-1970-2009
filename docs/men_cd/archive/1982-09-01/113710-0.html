More than 80 kinds of gooseberries and currants grow wild in North America. The ripe round fruit can vary in size, color, and — to some degree — taste and might be either spiny or smooth-skinned. Early fall's the time to go a-gathering . . . and when you do, be sure to pick enough to make one of life's most toothsome culinary concoctions: a fresh gooseberry pie!
