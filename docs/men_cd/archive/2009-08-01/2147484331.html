Recipes For Refreshing Face Masks

Make a refreshing facial mask with common kitchen products.

By Pamela Koontz

August/September 2009

Did you know you can make a refreshing facial mask from items found in your pantry and refrigerator? The benefits are threefold: saving money, protecting the environment by not using chemical-laden products, and caring for your skin naturally!

Here are recipes for two of my favorite homemade face masks:

Farmerâ€™s Wife Face Mask

2 chilled egg whites
1/8 tsp cornstarch

Whip the egg whites until they form peaks, then slowly add the cornstarch. Apply the mixture immediately to your face, leaving it on for 20 minutes. Rinse your face with cool water.

Glorious Green Face Mask

1 mashed avocado
1/3 cup honey
1/2 cup oatmeal

In a bowl, mix the mashed avocado with the honey, then add oatmeal. When all ingredients are mixed well, apply to your face and relax for 15 to 20 minutes. Wash the mask off with cool water.
