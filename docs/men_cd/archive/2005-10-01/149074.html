Mother Earth News' Publisher On Diy Tv Show

Publisher Bryan Welch hit the small screen to showcase new products for green building.

By the Mother Earth News editors

October/November 2005

In July, Mother Earth News Publisher Bryan Welch made a television appearance on “Today’s Homeowner with Danny Lipford.”

Welch showcased several new green building products on Lipford’s show, which is nationally syndicated to more than 190 TVstations.

For more information about the show, visit www.dannylipford.com.
