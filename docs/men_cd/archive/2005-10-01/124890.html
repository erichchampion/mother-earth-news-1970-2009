Putting Local Food On The Table

October/November 2005

Many people are discovering that farmer's markets are great placesto buy fresh, delicious produce, but farmers are finding even moreways to bring high-quality local and organic food to the public.'Farm to School' programsin 17 states are making connections between local farmers andschool lunch programs, giving farmers a market for their produce,and bringing healthy food into school cafeterias.

Many restaurants are also buying from local farmers, in an effortto get fresher, higher quality ingredients on their menus. A goodplace to start looking for restaurants selling local foods is theChefsCollaborative, an organization that connects restaurants withlocal farmers and that has partners in 36 states.
