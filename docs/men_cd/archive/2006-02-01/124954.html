Home Work: Handbuilt Shelter

February/March 2006

Looking for the cure to the common home? You'll find all theinspiration you'll need about unique, handmade and offbeat shelterin Lloyd Kahn's newest book, Home Work: Handbuilt Shelter.Packed with 1,100 photos and 300 illustrations of dwellings, domes,houses and huts from all points around the globe, this remarkable245-page book is about the eclectic materials used to make thesehomes and the eccentric dreamers and doers that built them.

If you dream of building your own home, or you just like unusualand inspiring buildings, Home Work is about as entertaininga companion as you can find.
