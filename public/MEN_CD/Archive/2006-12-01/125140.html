'An Inconvenient Truth' Dvds Free To Teachers

December/January 2006

StopGlobalWarming.orghas begun donating DVD copies of Al Gore's documentary, 'AnInconvenient Truth,' to teachers looking to increase awareness ofthe effects of global warming to their students. 50,000 copies areavailable on a first come, first serve basis. VisitParticipate.net to signup.

The giveaway has already begun but will continue to until Thursday,January 18, 2007. The DVDs will be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks; afree curriculum guide can also be downloaded for extra assistance.If you aren't a teacher, consider contacing one who will takeadvantage of this incredible opportunity to educate Americanstudents about the dangers of global warming.
