Shave And A Hair Cut, Two Bits

By Randy Cobb

June/July 2000

A concern to all people who use a razor is the life of the blade. If the user would store the razor, between uses, in a plastic jar with a lid containing a 1/2" of isopropyl alcohol, you could get twice the amount of comfortable shaves in per blade. This would prevent most discomfort and some infections. The alcohol displaces H 2 0 and disinfects.

Randy Cobb
Santa Ana, CA
