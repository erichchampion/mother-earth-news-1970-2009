Clear Savings

Save money on household cleaning by buying windshield cleaner in the automotive section.

By Marilyn Gill

October/November 1998

COUNTRY LORE

by Marilyn Gill

Rather than pay high prices for window cleaners, shop the automotive section in your favorite store. Windshield cleaner is made to remove road grime and dried bugs, so it's up to most any household window cleaning problem, and it can be bought on sale for as little as 69C a gallon.

-Marilyn Gill
Lewistown, MT
