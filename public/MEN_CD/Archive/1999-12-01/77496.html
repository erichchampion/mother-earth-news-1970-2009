Clever Composting

December/January 1999

A 10' length of 3" perforated ADS flex pipe costs $3 to $4. It can be laid in a horizontal "U," or else run up through the pile, again in a U formation. The pipe will also disperse water through the pile.

The ADS flex pipe will take almost any abuse, including jabs from the compost fork, and should last a lifetime.

Dan D. Lion
Prather, California
