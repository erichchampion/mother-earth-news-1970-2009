Poison Ivy Prevention

Washing well with oil-busting dish soap, immediately after exposure, will remove the poison ivy oil from your skin.

By Marc Silhavy

June/July 2009

My doctor provided this tip for preventing poison ivy. We all know to wash after working outside, but I assumed using regular hand soap was fine. But instead, use dish soap to remove all of the poison ivy, as dish soap is made to remove grease and oils, which includes poison ivy oil.
