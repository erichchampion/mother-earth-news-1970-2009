Solar Investments Soar

June/July 2005

2004 may be remembered as the 'watershed year' for solar energy,and 2005 may be an even better year, according to a report by theProgressive Investor. In 2004, shares of the world's 24 publiclytraded solar power companies jumped by nearly 185 percent. Expertssay the solar photovoltaics (PV) market will grow from $7 billionto $30 billion by 2010. Solar PV, with its annual growth rateexceeding 30 percent, is about to become 'one of the world'sfast-growth, profitable' industries, according to the newsletter.Among the companies mentioned are Japan's Sharp, Germany'sSolarWorld and U.S.-basedEvergreen Solar.
