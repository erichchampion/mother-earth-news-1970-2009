Build Mother's Tool Sharpener

This project is easy enough for almost anyone to tackle with confidence, and will produce a piece of equipment that will keep your workshop running smoothly for years.

November/December 1981

In MOTHER NO. 70-on page 28-we ran an article that revealed some of the trade secrets used by our research crew to keep their inventory of bladed tools razor sharp. And-although the article itself was quite thorough-several of our readers have requested that we provide more details about the belt sander/sharpener described in that piece. So, in answer to these inquiries, here's an illustration that should help to clear up any questions regarding its assembly.
