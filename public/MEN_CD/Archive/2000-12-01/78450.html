Heavy Hitters

December/January 2000

When it comes to value the bigger panel is the better buy.

Utility-intertie systems are a snap with Evergreen Solar's EverSun AC Module package, which can be plugged directly into your home power supply and produces between 90 and 200 watts (depending upon model choice). EverSun systems retail for between $1,000 and $2,200.

The Siemens SR-100 module kicks out 100 watts peak power at 17-volts and retails for just over $600. A high-output bargain.

Unisolar's SHR-17 PV Shingle can turn a normal, run of the mill roof into a home power station. Each 86" x 12" shingle produces 17 watts at 8.6 volts and retails for $139.

Astropower's AP-120, at 120 watts peak output at 16.9 volts, is one of the most powerful residential modules available. It retails for about $700.
